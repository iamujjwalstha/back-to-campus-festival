The Concept

Food, Community,
Competition.

Senior students run international street food stalls at the Back to Campus Festival. New and returning students eat, explore, and vote with their wallets.

👨‍🍳 5

Nationalities

India, Nepal, China, Vietnam & Finland — each stall run by senior students from that culture, serving authentic fast food.

📶 50%

First Bite Discount

Show your wristband barcode at any stall. Your very first order is half price — making it risk-free to try something new.

🏆 1st

Champion Stall

After closing, all stall revenues are tallied. The highest earner is crowned champion — live, in front of the crowd.
